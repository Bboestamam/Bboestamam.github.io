Thinking of Selling?


Let us take care of this process with our white glove service as our team will provide a game plan to help you pocket the most amount in least amount of time stress free with clarity.

1. Introduction:

Let’s sit down and discuss what your real estate goals are for the future and your current situation as well as educating you on the current market conditions.

2. Home Tour:

Now that you are ready to list your home its time to get it ready for viewings! The first step is taking us and our stager through a detailed home tour where you will highlight any upgrades you may have done, that way we can emphasis these details to the new prospective buyers. During this tour we are also going to recommend areas where you should de personalize and de clutter, this helps the buyer envision themselves in the home.

3. Marketing:

Marketing is one of the most important factors in getting your home sold for top dollar. We want to get your home in front of the most amount of eyes as possible.

4. Pricing

No matter how amazing the photos come out and how well the stager made your home look, PRICE is important. Market dictates the price of your home. We will provide you with a Market Analysis of other homes in the area. What is currently on the market and what is sold. From there we can strategize a price to trigger and attract buyers to bring their agent and view your property and to provide your home with the most amount of exposure as possible!

Ways we market your home:


Professional Photos/Video:

One of the firsts steps in marketing a home is getting professional photos and a virtual tour of your home. We then use those photos for our print marketing and online advertisements.

Print Marketing:

For all our listings our marketing team will create a feature booklet for your property highlighting all the areas. So when they are shopping around other homes they have something to remind them of your home and all the unique characteristics.

Online Advertising:

Most home searches in todays market are online, which is why are so heavily focused on this aspect. We use targeted Facebook, Instagram and Google ads to get as much attention to your property as we can. With online advertising you are able to target certain areas, demographics and drive those online audiences to your home. We also send your home to an extensive list of our clients and other agents in our database.

Exclusive Neighbours Only Open House:

The purpose of this is to bring more awareness to your home. We will personally go door to door letting your neighbours know of the hottest new listing in their neighbourhood. We’ve often found that neighbours have friends and family looking to move closer to them for numerous reasons. Your house may also be the perfect up size or downsize for them!

Public Open House:

Once the home is listed we will have a public open house advertised on the listing for everyone that is seeing your home online. Many people work long hours during the week and weekend open houses are a great way for them to view numerous homes on the weekend. By having us there at the open house we are able to take the buyers through the home and emphasis the upgrades you have noted to us.
