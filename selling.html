Thinking of Selling?


Proceed below to see how we can help!

Intro

We would discuss what it important to you and get to know what’s your goal and situation for you to sell and discuss the market conditions.

Home Tour

We take a walk through of your home in a buyers view. This is a great opportunity to give us a tour of your home and to know what upgrades & highlights your home has. We will also, advise what is needed to be de-personalized and declutter so your home can show well.

Marketing

We will discuss your home and what is need to be done. By removing all clutter and personal items from the space and organizing closets and drawers. Make sure your home is as clean as possible, and make any updates. To give you a hand, we’ll schedule a complimentary home-staging assessment and connect you with our professional stager. To give last minute touches before our photographer and marketing team create magic, for our marketing package for us to Door knock for our wine and cheese open house, public open house and to advertise to our database your home!

Price

No matter how amazing the photos come out and how well the stager made your home to look, PRICE is important. Market dictates the price of your home. We will provide you with a Market Analysis of other homes in the area, what is currently on the market and what is sold. From there we can strategize a price to trigger and attract buyers to bring their agent and view your property and to provide your home with the most amount of exposure as possible!
